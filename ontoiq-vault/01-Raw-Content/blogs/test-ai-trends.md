# Test Blog: AI Trends 2026

## Introduction
Artificial Intelligence is evolving rapidly in 2026. Here are the key trends:

## Key Trends

### 1. Multi-Agent Systems
Multi-agent systems are becoming mainstream, allowing AI agents to collaborate on complex tasks.

### 2. Tool Use Protocols
AI agents can now use external tools and APIs effectively, expanding their capabilities.

### 3. Long-Context Models
Models with longer context windows enable better understanding of complex documents.

### 4. Reasoning Improvements
New architectures show significant improvements in logical reasoning and problem-solving.

### 5. Safety Frameworks
AI safety is becoming a priority with new frameworks for responsible AI development.

## Conclusion
These trends will shape how we interact with AI in the coming years.
